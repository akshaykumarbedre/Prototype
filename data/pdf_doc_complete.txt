## INTEGRATED SMARTCARD AND TRANSACTION

### Cards
The student card will have a built-in wallet for all campus-related activities. Students can use it for payments, entry to restricted areas, marking attendance, and borrowing books from the library.

### Transactions
All transactions will work offline, and the data will be saved directly on the card. The POS machines will only need to connect to the internet once a day to sync the data.

### POS
POS (Point of Sale) machines will be set up at every counter. These machines will be ready to use and linked to the student cards.

### Reports
Detailed reports (MIS Reports) will be automatically created in the system and sent to the concerned authorities daily. These reports will show all important transaction and usage data.
